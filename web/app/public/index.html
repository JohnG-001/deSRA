Differential expression for SRA data

An automated protocol to extract differences in gene expression from public NGS datasets.

Please enter comma separated Gene names and SRA accessions for each condition. Also enter an email address to get a notification when your job is done, as this tool will take a while to run. deSRA uses human genome assembly GRCh38 and the most recent annotation release for gene sequences. Read our documentation at https://github.com/NCBI-Hackathons/deSRA.
